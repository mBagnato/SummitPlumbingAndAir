Cooling / Air Conditioning Installation And Maintenance

We supply, install, and maintain all the big brand cooling systems. Our industry partnerships and access to trade pricing means we can often save our clients money on the recommended retail price of many units.:

Some of the factors we consider include:

  • Size of the room / space / building to be heated
  • Frequency of use (i.e. one person, a family of five, or a small business with hundreds of customers a day?)
  • Type of use (i.e. residential vs light commercial)
  • Budget and ongoing running costs
  • Energy rating - the higher the rating, the lower the running costs
  • Single or double storey and access required
  • Length of tenancy

Evaporative coolers are a smart choice for cooling on a budget.

They generally cost less than air conditioners in both purchase price and running costs and still do a great job keeping large spaces cool. Just keep in mind how it will be used, as they can be less effective in smaller spaces and on humid days. It's also not uncommon to hear the air being circulated, making it less ideal for homes or businesses demanding silence..

While Gas Ducted Heating units exist on their own, most people see the benefit in adding on the cooling function to ensure year-round enjoyment..

The heater still performs its heating function as it was designed to, however, the coolness you feel in your home is generated by a fan that blows air through a cooling coil before pushing it out through its vents. A remote controlled thermostat allows you to control the temperature.

Ducted split systems with reverse cycle technology provide cooling and heating through a central unit called an air handler.

This unit is connected to a condenser unit installed outside the building and the cooled or heated air is pushed through ducts in the ceiling to each room. The air temperature is generally consistent and the units are considered relatively quiet and discreet.

ENQUIRE NOW

Wall Mount Split systems are a popular choice for homes and businesses for a number of reasons.

They can be installed and used only in the areas needed, and with the option of both heating and cooling functions, their temperatures can be adjusted independently so everyone gets what they want! They also don’t require ductwork to be added which can save space and cost. Split systems work better in smaller spaces and should be maintained regularly to keep them running efficiently and effectively..

Cooling Comparisons

Evaporative

  • Budget-friendly
  • Easy to install
  • Designed for large spaces
  • Suitable for people with allergies due to the fresh air being drawn into the house
  • Air circulation is audible
  • Not ideal for humid environments
  • Because it’s not a refrigerated system, there is less control over the room temperature

Ducted Split

  • Quiet and discreet
  • Efficient and consistent temperature
  • More expensive to install
  • Repairs can be complex due to the number of components

Gas Ducted Heating With Add-On Cooler

  • Ducts allow for individual zones
  • Offers both heating and cooling for year- round enjoyment
  • Less expensive running costs than multiple systems
  • More expensive running costs than a ducted heater
  • More complex installation than other heating options

Wall Mount Split System

  • Install and operate in the spaces you want cooled
  • Operate individually
  • Affordable to purchase
  • Easy to install
  • Not great for large spaces
  • Not as compact or discreet as ducted
