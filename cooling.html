Cooling Comparisons

Evaporative

Evaporative cooling systems are a popular choice for those seeking an efficient and environmentally-friendly way to cool their homes or businesses. These systems work by drawing hot air from outside the building through wet cooling pads, which then evaporate the water and cool the air before distributing it throughout the space. One of the main benefits of evaporative cooling systems is their energy efficiency. Compared to traditional air conditioning units, which require a significant amount of energy to cool a space, evaporative cooling systems use up to 75% less energy. Additionally, because they rely on water rather than refrigerant, they are much better for the environment. Another advantage of evaporative cooling systems is their affordability. They are generally less expensive to purchase and install than traditional air conditioning units, making them a popular choice for those on a budget. They are also relatively easy to maintain, with simple and inexpensive cleaning procedures required to keep the cooling pads functioning properly. Overall, evaporative cooling systems offer a cost-effective and eco-friendly solution to cooling large spaces.

Ducted Split

A ducted split system provides efficient and even heating and cooling throughout the home, using a central unit to distribute conditioned air via ductwork. With discreet installation, convenient control via a central thermostat, and long-term cost savings, it is a great choice for homeowners who want a reliable and energy-efficient heating and cooling system.

Gas Ducted Heating With Add-On Cooler

This system combines gas ducted heating with an add-on cooler, making it an all-season solution. The gas ducted heating system provides efficient heating during winter while the add-on cooler can be used to cool the house during summer. The system is easy to use and can be controlled with a thermostat. It is also energy-efficient and can save on heating and cooling costs. However, it is more expensive than other cooling systems and requires regular maintenance. Overall, this system is a good option for those who want a year-round solution for their heating and cooling needs.

Wall Mount Split System

A Wall Mount Split System is a popular air conditioning option that can cool or heat a single room or area. It consists of two units - an indoor unit mounted on the wall and an outdoor unit placed outside the room. The indoor unit blows cool air into the room while the outdoor unit removes heat from the air. It is a cost-effective solution that is easy to install and energy-efficient, making it an attractive option for those who want to cool or heat a specific area without installing a complex ducted system. Additionally, Wall Mount Split Systems have a sleek design and offer quiet operation, making them a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
